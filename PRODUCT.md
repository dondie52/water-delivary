# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Fresh Water Market OS serves the full platform:

- **Customers** — campus students, Gaborone residents, and households ordering bottled water, refills, ice, and subscriptions via mobile-first web.
- **Corporate & event buyers** — offices, schools, and events purchasing bulk water, branded bottles, and recurring supply with account management.
- **Internal staff** — dispatch, inventory, production, CRM, and finance teams running daily operations from admin consoles.
- **Drivers** — field staff on phones managing routes, proof of delivery, and cash collection.

Each surface shares one brand, but the default design register is **product**: dashboards and workflows that earn trust through competence, not marketing spectacle.

## Product Purpose

Fresh Water Market OS is the operating system for bottled water retail, refill operations, branded bottle production, and delivery logistics across Botswana. It unifies customer ordering, commerce, fulfillment, inventory, CRM, corporate accounts, and analytics in one modular platform — built for pilot operations now and multi-branch expansion later.

Success looks like: customers order with confidence, staff move orders without friction, and the brand feels premium and local — never like a generic SaaS template.

## Brand Personality

**Premium, calm, trustworthy.**

Understated confidence. Clean hydration. The interface should feel as considered as the water — clear, fresh, and reliable. Voice is direct and helpful, not salesy or startup-hype. Visual energy comes from water and clarity, not decoration.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards — Stripe-clone admin layouts, purple gradients, identical icon+heading+text card grids.
- AI landing-page tropes — warm cream/beige body backgrounds, tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows on every section, numbered section markers (01 / 02 / 03) as default scaffolding.
- Cheap discount water brand — clip art, loud sales banners, bargain-bin visual language that undermines premium positioning.

## Design Principles

1. **Trust through clarity** — Every screen earns confidence by making the next action obvious. No decorative noise that competes with the job.
2. **Operations-first** — Admin, driver, and staff surfaces prioritize speed, scanability, and error prevention over visual flair.
3. **Local premium, not startup generic** — Feel like a Botswana water brand with real fulfillment behind it, not a YC demo day product.
4. **Mobile reality** — Drivers and customers live on phones; touch targets, one-handed flows, and reduced-motion respect field conditions.
5. **Show competence in fulfillment** — Surface order status, delivery windows, and inventory truthfully. Trust is built by what the system proves, not what the hero section claims.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **Mobile-first** — Primary interaction model for customers and drivers; responsive layouts are not an afterthought.
- **Reduced motion** — Honor `prefers-reduced-motion`; animations enhance but never gate content visibility.
- **Contrast & readability** — Body text meets WCAG 2.1 AA contrast; placeholder and muted text are not washed-out gray on tinted backgrounds.
- **Keyboard & focus** — Interactive elements remain reachable and visibly focused for staff on desktop consoles.
